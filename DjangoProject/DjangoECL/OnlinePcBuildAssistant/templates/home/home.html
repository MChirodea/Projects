How We Got Our Start


Like anyone else, we had to start from the bottom. We built our first PC's only after hours of tutorials and research in order to make sure all of the components were compatible with one and another. Sometimes, even hoping they would start. It's from that time we got the desire to help people so that they don't go through what we went. That's how Online PC Build Assistant was born.

A simple tool with a single job, help users create their PC's by making sure the parts are compatible.
